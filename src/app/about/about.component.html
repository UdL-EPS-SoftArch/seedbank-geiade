SeedBankGEIADE

The Seedbank Management System is a software application designed to manage and maintain the inventory of seeds in a seedbank. The system provides a user-friendly interface that allows users to easily add, remove, and track seeds within the seedbank.

The software includes advanced features such as batch upload and download of seed data, barcode scanning for easy tracking of seed inventory, and customizable reports for analyzing seed collection trends.
